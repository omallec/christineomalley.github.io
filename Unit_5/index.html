Overview:

Established in 2007, e-Learning Designs is a woman-owned business (registered with the Small Business Administration), focused on personalized support designed to enhance learning.

Offerings include:

  • Content Restructuring (classroom training to an online format)
  • Content Development in Lectora or Storyline
  • Lectora Tutoring for SMEs
  • Accessibility Support related to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)

Design and Development

Need to deliver a new course or convert instructor-led training to e-Learning? Looking for an experienced e-Learning Designer/Developer?

I develop content from your storyboard or restructure outdated, static text and transform it into learner-focused, interactive content designed to enhance your audience's ability to apply what they learn.

Tutoring for SMEs

Need to learn how to author content in Lectora? Not sure where to start or getting stuck?

I review your existing Lectora work and provide personalized guidance designed to enhance your knowledge and give you a jumpstart in creating well-organized, aesthetically pleasing, and properly coded content.

Accessibility Support (WCAG)

Need to make your e-Learning accessible? Not sure where to start or feeling a bit overwhelmed?

I provide collaborative support to Lectora authors through the Lectora Accessibility User Group (LAUG). You are invited to join the group and participate in open discussions designed to enhance your knowledge of WCAG.
